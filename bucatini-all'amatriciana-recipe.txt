Serves 4

Ingredients
 - 400g bucatini
 - 80g pecorino, freshly grated
 
For the sauce:
 - 130g guanciacle, cut into small cubes
 - 1 small onion, finely chopped
 - 3-4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
 - 1 hot dried red chilli, finely chopped
 - 2 tbsp dry white wine
 - 500g San Marzano tomatoes, cut into quarters
 
Method

 For the sauce, put the guanciacle and onion in a pan with the oil and chilli, and fry gently for about 4-5 minutes. Add the white wine and tomatoesand cook for a further 15-20 minutes, stirring occassionaly.
 
 Meanwhile, bring a large saucepan of lightly salted water to the boil, and cook the pasta until al dente. Strain the bucatini and mix with the sauce.
 Serce springkled generously with the freshly grated pecorino.
